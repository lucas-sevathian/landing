In Brief

Bac & Orientation was created by 4 students in their final year at the Jean Sturm high school in Strasbourg, ranked 7th nationally. Unlike other standard websites, our concept is to offer you courses, prepare you for competitive exams, and help you in your orientation via the Discord platform. That is why we decided to combine free lectures & low-cost courses, a rich experience, a 24H/7D availability, a centralized multidisciplinarity as well as a simplicity thought for all.


It is these characteristics that make it the only O.J. where success makes sense.

Functioning

Through the different channels, choose the subject in which you need help, then proceed to make an appointment, then agree with the teacher on your availability and the price list.


Bac & Orientation helps students at three levels through a Discord :


  •    Courses & Conferences :

Our classes are taught by high school students who know the curriculum and its expectations. These courses are intended for students from the 6th grade to higher education. You can prepare tests, homework, exercises and presentations. All of our teachers are fluent in several languages (English, Spanish or German), as well as many specialties and options in Première and Terminale (SES, Mathematics, Physics-Chemistry, SVT and Humanities...). Private lessons on the core curriculum are also offered. Free lectures will be regularly available for all and will allow you to obtain additional information concerning the different levels and their contents.

  •    Orientation :

The orientation assistance offered consists first of all of preparing students for post-bac / reorientation. We will also be at your disposal to help you in your choices of orientation whatever they are, in order to find you and make you integrate the school which corresponds to you. This means helping you with various exercises such as writing cover letters, creating CVs, Parcoursup information or preparing for oral interviews. Our teachers are specialized in business and medicine.


  •    Exams:

Preparation for competitive exams is also one of the principles of Bac & Orientation. They concern languages (TOEFL and Sprachdiplom) but also entrance exams to prestigious programs (Sesame and Medicine).
